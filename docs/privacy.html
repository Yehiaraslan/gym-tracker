Gym Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

Gym Tracker is a personal fitness tracking application. Your data stays on your device. We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to third parties.

Data We Collect

All workout data, body measurements, and app settings are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is sent to external servers unless you explicitly connect a third-party service.

WHOOP Integration

If you connect your WHOOP account, we access the following data via WHOOP's official API:

  • Recovery — recovery score, HRV, resting heart rate
  • Cycles — day strain, average heart rate
  • Sleep — sleep duration, start and end times
  • Workouts — activity type, strain
  • Profile — basic profile information
  • Body Measurements — height, weight, max heart rate

This data is fetched on-demand and cached locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. You can disconnect WHOOP at any time to revoke access.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal fitness data. WHOOP OAuth tokens are stored securely on-device.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the application. To revoke WHOOP access, disconnect from within the app or visit your WHOOP account settings.
